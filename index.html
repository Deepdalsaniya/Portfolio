03 — HOW I THINK

A data-driven problem solver.

My instinct is to treat every business question like a model: define the problem, find the cleanest data, pressure-test the assumptions, and ship something measurable. SQL, Power BI, and financial modeling are how I get there — not the point in themselves.

That same lens runs through a personal project: 4+ years actively trading US and Indian equities, building technical-analysis and options strategies, and increasingly applying AI to screen setups and structure research — turning market intuition into testable, data-backed decisions.
